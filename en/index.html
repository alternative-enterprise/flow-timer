Flow Timer

About the App

Flow Timer is a minimalist focus timer app based on the Pomodoro Technique. It helps you maintain a productive rhythm by alternating between work and break sessions. Multiple timer presets and sound notifications keep you on track throughout your day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the timer?

Tap the timer in the center of the screen to start a work session. When the work period ends, a sound notification will play and the timer will automatically switch to a break session. You can choose between "Quick (15min/3min)" and "Standard (25min/5min)" presets.

No sound is playing

Please check that the silent switch on your device is turned off and the volume is turned up. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

How can I remove ads?

You can remove ads by making a "Support the Developer" donation through the app.

How do I restore my purchase?

Tap the "Restore Purchases" button on the Settings screen. Your previous purchase will be restored as long as you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account.

What devices and OS versions are supported?

iOS 13 or later / Android 5.0 or later.

What languages are supported?

The app supports English, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified), Spanish, and Korean. It automatically adapts to your device's language setting.
